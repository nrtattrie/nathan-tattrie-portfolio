Slim Power Pack

Roam Robotics

Redesigned Roam's PowerPack from scratch with only 8 months to design, test, and manufacture. The original demo pack was fragile, oversized, and ran hot enough to melt components off the PCBs.

  • Reduced weight from 15.4 lbs to 9.7 lbs (37% reduction)
  • Used carbon fiber-reinforced 3D filament to meet drop, impact, and vibration requirements
  • Achieved water ingress sealing with 2mm o-ring stock and sealing fasteners
  • Maintained full airflow performance despite dramatic size reduction

Now the gold standard for what Roam's customers expect.

Compressor Investigation

Roam Robotics

Diagnosed and solved two interacting failure modes in a BLDC piston compressor: accumulator pressure leaking backward through reed valves causing idle "burp" cycles, and startup stalls from trapped cylinder pressure exceeding motor torque.

  • Redesigned reed valve plates to eliminate reverse leakage
  • Built a custom pony-brake torque fixture to map stall torque vs. pressure
  • Profiled motor constants across a statistically significant sample to rule out variability
  • Implemented controlled vent path with GPIO-driven valves for pressure relief at idle

Eliminated backflow, reduced stalls, improved battery life and reliability.
